Overview

Purpose

 I am a Computer Information Technology major and I like to work with computers. I have since become interested in building a computer for gaming, and productivity. At the moment prices are high and supply is low for computer parts due to the pandemic, but also competition. Sometimes customers in the market will ask themselves "Can I build an expensive computer without breaking something?" I have noticed that it is easier for companies to get these parts rather than the consumer. My company has a goal of acquiring computer components to build PCs for people's needs and wants. We will provide the customer multiple different options depending on computer parts with an added build fee for labor and time. Ultimately this will make it easier for the customer while building a more than capable computer for what they want!

Audience

 We hope here at MasterTech that we can appeal to a vast audience. Whether it be your first computer or your fifth, we can help you with what you need. We project that our audience will be vast, it will mainly compose of those young and young adult ages. Though we can help anyone with any of their needs that fit into their budget. Whether it be someone that needs a work PC or a gaming and streaming PC, we are here to help. This audience will have a sense of satisfaction and security as we build their computer with leading class experts and technicians.

Content

Home page

 This will be the face of MasterTech and the first thing people will see when they are curious about building a computer. It will be well set up, have a good color scheme, and be easily usable for anyone that visits. People will come to visit this site because they need to build a computer. Whether it be for a personal home PC or a large gaming and streaming rig. The market at the moment is tough to find computer parts due to a lack of supply, and an excess of demand. It can be daunting for a consumer to find these components or systems. Luckily, companies have better access and can provide for their consumers.

 We will provide competitive prices and a build fee plus tax. We feel that a computer nowadays is a need for everyone regardless of what you are doing. Especially with the recent pandemic, it is almost impossible to survive without one for work, school, or various hobbies. We believe here at MasterTech that we will be one of the best in the market for PC building. We will provide a streamlined experience with competitive prices that you cannot beat anywhere else.

Images for the Home page

Build Your PC

 This is the most important portion of the site. It is where every customer will go to build their computer. On this portion of the site, we will provide many options based on the consumer's needs. It will be easily navigated and will provide sufficient descriptions and prices. We will also provide pictures so that the consumer knows exactly what they are getting.

 We first will offer a basic home desktop option for someone who needs a computer for everyday computer use. This person will just use the computer for basic web browsing, school, and work. The next desktop we will offer is the beginner series. This desktop will be good for those who want to get into PC gaming, but do not know where to start. This customer will plan on playing lighter games, photo, and video editing. Next up is the intermediate series. This is for those who want a little more power and are willing to pay a bit more. This computer will be capable of playing e-sports titles at high framerates and some higher AAA games at mid to low settings. The next line we will offer is the advances series. This will be the best of the best you can find. Though it will not be cheap, it will be powerful and more than capable of whatever you throw at it. It can play any game at high settings and work through video and photo rendering with ease. Lastly, we will offer an option for a customer to customize and build their computer based on the components we already have. This customer already knows what they want or have specific needs, and we can satisfy them.

FAQ

 Now buying a computer can be a bit of a process sometimes. It sometimes is a huge investment, and people will have questions about their product or the process of building their PC. Some examples of questions they will ask will be “How long does it take to build and ship my PC?” or “What kind of computer should I get?” Buying a computer will sometimes feel like buying a car. You have to shop around for what you like and need, and it takes more time than usual. We hope we can expedite that process and make it happen with ease. We will also have a place to ask questions that we can answer promptly.
